ABOUT ME

I graduated with a degree in art history
from Cardinal Stefan Wyszynski
University in Warsaw in 2015.

I also have an art degree after
graduating from art high school (ZPSP)
in Warsaw.

My focus is in illustration, 2d animation
and game design.

I want to learn and improve all the time,
because I just love drawing.
